Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, selling, and earning on Campus Plug.

How does Campus Plug work?

Campus Plug is a dual-platform marketplace for Ghanaian students. You can buy and sell items (textbooks, electronics, fashion, dorm essentials) in the Marketplace, or post and bid on freelance gigs (tutoring, graphic design, delivery, event help). Payments are held securely in escrow via Mobile Money until both parties are satisfied.

Is it free to use?

Absolutely. Creating an account, browsing listings, posting items, and bidding on gigs are all 100% free. Campus Plug only collects a 10% platform fee on completed sales — if nothing sells, you pay nothing.

How do payments work?

All payments are processed through Paystack using Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money). When you buy an item or hire a freelancer, your payment is held in secure escrow. The seller only receives the funds after you confirm the item is delivered or the gig is completed. This protects both buyers and sellers.

Can I trust other students on the platform?

We encourage you to check a seller's ratings and peer reviews before transacting. Our escrow system ensures your money is only released when you confirm satisfaction. We also recommend meeting in safe public places on campus to inspect items. Our support team is available at {{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }} to help resolve any disputes.

What if something goes wrong with my order?

If you have an issue with a transaction, you can open a dispute from your payments dashboard. An admin will review the case and release or refund the funds appropriately. For any other concerns, reach out to us at {{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }} and we will respond promptly.

Who can join Campus Plug?

Anyone 16 years or older can join — you don't need to be a student at a specific campus. Whether you're at KNUST, University of Ghana, Ashesi, UCC, or a business owner looking to reach students, you're welcome to buy, sell, and hire on Campus Plug.
